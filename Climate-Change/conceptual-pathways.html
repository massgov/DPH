Conceptual Pathways for Climate-Related Health Outcomes

Climate Drivers Hazard Exposures Vulnerable Groups Health Risks
Climate Driver: Heat – Hazard: Heat Events
  • More frequent
  • Longer duration
  • More extreme
 Exposures: Elevated temperatures Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 5
  • Individuals with:
    • Diabetes
    • Cardiovascular disease
    • Renal system disease
    • Outdoor workers
    • Outdoor recreation
 Health Risks:
  • Heat Exhaustion
  • Heat Stroke
  • Illness or death due to cardiovascular disease
  • Illness or death due to renal system failure
Hazard: Poor Outdoor Air Quality
  • increased generation of ozone
  • increased pollen production
  • longer pollen season
 Exposures: Elevated levels of ozone Vulnerable Groups:
  • Outdoor workers
  • Outdoor recreation
  • Individuals with:
  • Asthma
  • COPD
  • Lung Disease
  • Respiratory allergies
  • Cardiovascular disease
 Health Risks:
  • Respiratory arrest/death
  • Exacerbation of asthma and other lung disease
  • Exacerbation of allergies and respiratory infections
  • Reduction of childhood lung function growth
Exposures: Elevated levels of pollen Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals with allergies
  • Individuals with asthma
 Health Risks:
  • Exacerbation of asthma
  • More frequent allergy symptoms for longer periods of the year
Hazard: Food-borne Illness Exposures: Spoiled food from refrigeration failure in transport/storage Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Gastrointestinal illness
Exposures: Changes in patterns of salmonella growth Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Salmonella infection
Climate Driver: Heat and Precipitation Changes – Hazard: Disease Carrying Pests
  • Longer exposure seasons
  • Expanding geographical ranges
  • Increased habitat
  • Higher breeding rates
Exposures: Ticks Vulnerable Groups:
  • Outdoor workers
  • Outdoor recreation
  • Individuals with pets that go outdoors
 Health Risks:
  • Lyme Disease
  • Babesiosis
Exposures: Mosquitoes Vulnerable Groups:
  • Outdoor workers
  • Outdoor recreation
  • Individuals over age 50
  • Individuals under age 15
 Health Risks:
  • West Nile Virus
  • Eastern Equine Encephalitis
Hazard: Water-borne illnesses Exposures: Water or shellfish contaminated with Vibrio Vulnificus Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Gastrointestinal illness
Exposures: Contact with e Coli contaminated water from runoff, flooding or sewer overflow discharge Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Gastrointestinal Illness
  • Urinary tract infection
Climate Driver: Precipitation Changes – Hazard: Flooding Exposures: Contaminated flood water (direct contact or flood-contaminated drinking water) Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Gastrointestinal illness
  • Urinary tract infection
Exposures: Debris/Physical hazards Vulnerable Groups:
  • Adolescents
  • Young adults
  • Utility workers
  • Rescue workers
 Health Risks:
  • Injury, drowning, electrocution
Exposures: Relocation/Displacement Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Individuals with mobility limitations
  • Individuals under age 18
  • Individuals with low socioeconomic status
  • Individuals with low English language fluency
  • Non-white persons
  • Immigrants
  • Renters
 Health Risks:
  • Psychological stress
  • Financial loss stress
  • Loss of employment
  • Alcohol/drug abuse
Exposures: Mold/mildew Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals with immune system or respiratory issues
  • Individuals with Allergies
  • Individuals with Asthma
 Health Risks:
  • Respiratory illness
Hazard: Drought Exposures: Water quality degradation Vulnerable Groups:
  • Individuals over age 65
  • Children under age 6
  • Pregnant women
  • Individuals with compromised immune systems
 Health Risks:
  • Gastrointestinal Illness
  • Heavy metal poisoning
Exposures: Sinkholes/Land Subsidence Vulnerable Groups:
  • All people
Health Risks:
  • Physical Injuries/death
  • Psychological stress
Exposures: Loss of green spaces Vulnerable Groups:
  • All peple
 Health Risks:
  • Heat-related illnesses
