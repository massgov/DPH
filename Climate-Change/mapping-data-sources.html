    For most of the demographic data, we used the 2010 decennial census. The American Community Survey (ACS) 5- year estimates (2006-2010) were used for Percent pf Population Living without High School Education and Percent of Population Living at or Below Poverty. The estimates are based on 60 months of collected data and are considered to be the most reliable when compared to the 1– and 3–year estimates that are based on 12 and 36 months of data respectively. Specific information on the sources of the data and methods for each indicator are available using American Factfinder (http://factfinder.census.gov/) and summarized below.
